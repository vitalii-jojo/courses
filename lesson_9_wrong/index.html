What‚Äôs a Tribe?
In organizational context, a Tribe is a larger group of teams in a company who work in related areas or on related projects. This term is often used in Agile development and was popularized by Spotify in their well-known model for scaling Agile. In the Spotify model, a Tribe is a collection of teams, known as Squads, that work in the same business area. For example, all Squads that work on the user experience could form a Tribe. The idea is to encourage collaboration and communication across related teams while maintaining each team's autonomy. It's important to note that the concept of Tribes can vary between organizations, and not all companies use this structure.

The process of inviting someone will depend on the platform or service you're using.
